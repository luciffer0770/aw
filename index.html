Our Story

Born from a love of
coffee & cream

AVAYA began as a weekend experiment in a Bangalore kitchen — a dream to merge the boldness of espresso with the silky comfort of cheesecake. What started as jars for friends and family quickly turned into something the city couldn't stop talking about.

Every jar is handcrafted in small batches, layered with intention, and sealed with care. No shortcuts. No compromise. Just real ingredients and a whole lot of love.
